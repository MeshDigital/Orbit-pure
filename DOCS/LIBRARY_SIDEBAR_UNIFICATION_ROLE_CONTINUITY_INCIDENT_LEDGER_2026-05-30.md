# Library Sidebar Unification - Role Continuity Incident Ledger (2026-05-30)

## Purpose

Capture role continuity incidents and their fixes to preserve operator handoff reliability.

## Ledger Fields

- Incident timestamp and wave
- Affected surface (contract, index, status, recap, queue)
- Impact severity
- Corrective action performed
- Prevention tactic added to memory

## Retention

Keep entries through at least the next two completed 10-slice waves.
