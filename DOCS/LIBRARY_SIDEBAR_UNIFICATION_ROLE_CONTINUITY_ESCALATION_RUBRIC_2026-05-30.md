# Library Sidebar Unification - Role Continuity Escalation Rubric (2026-05-30)

## Purpose

Define when continuity concerns should escalate from routine maintenance to governance-level review.

## Rubric Levels

- Level 1: Minor wording drift, no contract impact
- Level 2: Surface mismatch across index/status/recap
- Level 3: Contract assertions and published artifacts diverge
- Level 4: Repeated divergence across consecutive waves

## Response Pattern

- Levels 1-2: Fix in current wave and document in recap
- Levels 3-4: Halt queue advancement until contract and planning alignment is restored

## Outcome

Predictable escalation rules reduce ambiguity during high-frequency slice execution.
